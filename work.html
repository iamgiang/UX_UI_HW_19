My Work

Xtravagari mobile travel app

UX case study Xtravagari is a travel app that caters to the needs of the modern traveller by aggregating COVID-19 safety information from around the world and attractions within a centralised location.

Xtravagari considers travel pain points to accommodate the modern travellers’ safety needs and allows access to users own travelling habits as well as other travellers’ content to provide authentic recommendation.

The Australian Navy website redesign

The Royal Australian Navy website has an informative purpose. Users are able to check news, the fleet, the culture, the history of the Australian Navy and also support for marine families. Additionally, the website serves as a recruitment platform.

As a user case study, the Australian Government Navy website reimagined in a responsive redesign from user pain points using user interface analysis (proto-persona, usability test interview, heuristic evaluation, card sorting, sitemap) and information architecture (wireframing & prototype).

Act Now! responsive website design

Act Now! is a social activist platform that allows users to keep up to date and be informed on political and social events happening around them. Also allows users options to get involved and contribute with friends, through challenges and donations.

Designed a responsive website through research, definition, ideation, wireframes, prototyping, user testing and ideation.
